About THE

project

Royal plus wants to showcase their products, brands, and innovation (OEM, ODM, and OBM). Thus, they want the look and feel of the website to be modern and innovative, yet colorful like their source of natural ingredients. Though, this website’s purpose is to inform about the business and product details. They put variety of drinks within their own brands. Thus, we use a lot of photos to show the products’ packagings, ingredients, etc. As we use mostly nature colors like green and brown for the design, we use bright colors photos with tropical elements.

*The work was designed by me while working at Optiwise Co.ltd.
